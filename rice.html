Rice is a staple food of South India. It is a main dish in most of the homes on the daily menu.

There are more than 5 kinds of rice varieties which are usually combined with some gravy(veg/non-veg) or rasam.

Instead of such combinations, one can make flavored rice which adds nutrient values to the rice, according to the quantity of addition.

Coconut rice is a rarely prepared recipe but a health-packed food. Packed with antooxident and anti bacterial effects, coconut rice makes it a nutrient filled dish.

Preparation Time: 10-15mints
Ingredients:
Oil
Mustard
Cumin
Peanuts
Chana dal
Urad dal
Dry chili
Green Chili
Coconut
Salt
Asafoetida
Rice
Cooking Method:In a hot pan, heat a few drops of oil before adding cumin, and mustard seeds. Add peanuts, chana dal, and urad dal to the seeds. Join a few red, and green chilies, along with curry leaves, and asafoetida. Put in salt and grated coconut, before adding the rice and mix well.

Lemon rice is a go-to recipe when there is leftover rice. It is prepared in many Hindu temples as an offering to deities and to distribute to devotees. It is also widely prepared as a snack when going on a picnic.

Preparation Time: 10-15mints
Ingredients:
Oil
Mustard
Cumin
Peanuts
Chana dal
Urad dal
Dry chili
Green Chili
Lemon
Turmeric
Salt
Asafoetida
Rice
Cooking Method:In a hot pan, heat a few drops of oil before adding cumin, and mustard seeds. Add peanuts, chana dal, and urad dal to the seeds. Join a few red, and green chilies, along with curry leaves, and asafoetida. Put in salt and turmeric, before squeezing a lemon. Add the lemon juice mixed rice into this mixture and mix well. Check for sourness and add lemon juice, accordingly.
Special Tips (if any): One can add onions also to the dals mixture, before adding chilies.



Jeera rice is a savory food when one wants to have a bowl of flavored rice packed with health. Shahi jeera used in jeera rice, is usually known for digestion. It is also used to treat coldness like cold/cough.
Preparation Time: 15-20mints
Ingredients:
Oil
Shahi Jeera
Dry chili
Green Chili
Onion
Mint
Salt
Rice
Cooking Method:In a hot pan, heat a few drops of oil and add shahi jeera. Join a few onions, red, and green chilies, mint leaves, and asafoetida. Pour water and sprinkle salt. Soon the water comes to a boil, add rice while stirring to prevent lumps. Boil rice until cooked.Special Tips (if any):



Bagara rice is a party food in many South Indian households. It is the first dish one prepares during any function/party/festival for the main course. Accommodating it with any curry makes it even more finger-licking.
Preparation Time:20-30minutes
Ingredients:
Species
Onion
Green chili
Ginger-garlic paste
Mint
Salt
Rice
Cooking Method:Add bay leaf, shahi jeera, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon to the hot oil. Join in onions, green chili, and salt. Put in ginger-garlic paste and fry until the raw smell is disappeared. Add mint leaves and pour in some water. Soon the water comes to a boil, add in the rice, soaked for 30 minutes, while stirring to avoid lumps. Boil the rice until cooked.
