With the wide variety of vegetables and spices available, one can prepare the same vegetable in different ways using various ingredients combinations.

Below are a few such veg recipes that are prepared in one of a kind.




Preparation Time: 30 minutes
Ingredients:
Potato
Oil
Mustard
Onion
Green Chili
Green peas
Tomato
Species (Salt, Chili powder, Turmeric, Mutton Masala, and Coriander powder)
Coriander
Cooking Method:In a hot pan, add oil and bring it to heat. Add mustard and let them splutter. Add onions, green chili, and green peas, and saute until soft. Add potatoes and cook until half boiled. Add tomatoes, salt, and turmeric, and cook until tomatoes become mushy. Add chili powder, coriander powder, and mutton masala with a little water and cook until the oil floats. Sprinkle coriander leaves.
